Flavors

Pot-O-Gold

Buttery goodness without the butter! This healthy alternative to the classic popcorn staple leaves it dairy-free as it's drizzled with our "Liquid Gold" sauce made from natural coconut oil. Topped with a sprinkling of Himalayan salt.

Pink Poppy

This pink delight is the taste of summer! This sweet treat is drizzled with a sauce made of creamy vanilla and strawberry fruit flavor.

Shamrock Shock

This green popcorn is a candy lovers favorite! Topped with lucky charms, pop-rocks, and a sweet and sour coating, this popcorn is sure to make your mouth POP!

Snicker Cookie

This treat will remind you of the smell of fresh-out-of-the-oven Snickerdoodle cookies! With it's coating of cinnamon sugar and bit of cookie crumble this pocorn is sure to satisfy any sweet-tooth.
