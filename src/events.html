Events

Lucky Pop will be attending various events throughout the 2024 year.
Here's a list of said events, hope to see you there!
Check out the events below!
Ogden Farmer's Market

Historic 25th Street Ogden, UT 84401

8:00 am - 1:00 pm, Every Saturday, from May to September

The Ogden Farmer's Market boasts farm fresh food such as fruits and veggies, as well as artisan breads. It offers live music, several art vendors, and food.

Ogden Farmer's Market Website

Brigham City Peach Days

1st South Main Street, Brigham City, UT 84302

10:00 am - 5:00 pm, Thursday September 5th - Saturday September 7th 2024

Peach Days celebrates the harvest of peaches in early September. Peach Days offers entertainment with a morning parade, car and motorcycle show, and several food trucks and vendors.

Peach Days Website

Vintage Market Days

Cache County Fairgrounds, 450 South 500 West Logan, UT

10:00 am - 5:00pm, Thursday May 9th - Saturday May 11th

Vintage Market Days offers several vendors showing off their unique antiques available for purchase. Some vendors even handcraft their own items for decor like never before seen. It also boasts food and live music.

Vintage Market Days Website
