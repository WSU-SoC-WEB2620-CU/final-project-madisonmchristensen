About

Hello, my name is Madison and I'm the owner of Lucky Pop. I created Lucky Pop because I simply love popcorn! I always loved making popcorn for my friends and family with my trusty air-popper topped with butter and sprinkled with salt. However, that all changed when I was diagnosed with SIBO (Small Intestine Bacteria Overgrowth) because of this my body could not take certain foods especially dairy and soy. I longed for the creamy butter taste on freshly popped popcorn. That's when I started experimenting with different recipes for making butter alternatives that still have that buttery taste. Once I nailed the recipe I decided that just as I loved making popcorn for friends and family with butter now I felt like this was something special to be shared with everyone and therefore, Lucky Pop was born.

I hope you enjoy our popcorn and have a lucky day!
~ Madison
